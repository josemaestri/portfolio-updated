About Me

I like people. I like music. I like fun. I don't do boring. I am a simple man who is easily amused. The world is my playground. The Internet is my sandbox. I use it to work, to play, to learn, and to connect. I try to bring that out in my work. The web should reflect the world around us, not just the one in our screens.

I am but a humble developer and designer. Always learning, and always with the zeal and enthusiasm of a pupil eager to further develop his craft. I will never claim to know it all (and am wary of those who do), but I will never back down from a challenge; the proverbial comfort zone scares me more than the unknown. And to be honest, anyone who doesn't share that fear with me doesn't stick around for long.

So let's talk and make something. Let's push comfort zones. Let's reflect the world around us.
